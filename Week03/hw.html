Egg Salad Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 3 tablespoons Mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice (optional)
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • 2 slices Bread

Instructions

  1. Boil the eggs for 10-12 minutes.
  2. Transfer to ice water, then peel and chop.
  3. Mix eggs with mayo, mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread on toasted bread and serve.
