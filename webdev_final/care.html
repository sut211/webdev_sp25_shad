Goldfish Care Guide

Goldfish
Care Guide

Feeding

Offer small portions 2-3 x day; remove leftovers after 2 min.

Water Change

Swap 30% water weekly to keep nitrates low.

Filtration

Turn-over 6-8x tank volume per hr for crystal clear water.

Tank Size

20-30 gal for the first fish + 10 gal each extra.

Live Plants

Anubias and Hornwort survive nibbles and lower toxins.

Methylene Blue

Your one-stop dip against fungus and parasites.

Quarantine

Isolate new fish 2 weeks to avoid tank-wide outbreaks.

Temperature

Ideally 68 - 74 °F (20 - 23 °C) for fancy breeds.

Aeration

Add an air-stone; goldies gulp more O₂ than most fish.

Enrichment

Substrate and hiding spots keep curious fish active.

Water Tests

Ammonia and nitrite must read 0 ppm — test weekly.
