Popular Goldfish Varieties

Goldfish come in many shapes, sizes, and color patterns. Below are six of the most common types you'll encounter.

Common Goldfish

The most widespread variety, known for its slender body shape, and vibrant orange coloration. They're hardy and relatively easy to care for.

Ranchu Goldfish

Often called the “King of Goldfish,” Ranchus have a curved back and a deep, rounded body. They're prized for their distinctive hood.

Oranda Goldfish

Recognizable by the fleshy cap (wen) on their heads. Orandas need special attention to keep their wen clean and free from infection.

Comet Goldfish

Similar to the common goldfish but with a longer, more deeply forked tail. Active swimmers, often recommended for ponds.

Telescope Goldfish

Known for their protruding, telescope-like eyes. They require gentle handling and a tank free of sharp objects.

Fantail Goldfish

A popular fancy variety with a split, fan-shaped tail. They are hardy and a good choice for beginners wanting a fancy goldfish.
